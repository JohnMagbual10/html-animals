GIraffes

Giraffes are the tallest land animals on Earth. Adult males can reach a height of up to 18 feet (5.5 meters), while females are slightly shorter.

Lions

Lions belong to the species Panthera leo and are part of the Felidae family. There are two main subspecies: the African lion (Panthera leo leo) and the Asiatic lion (Panthera leo persica).

Monkeys

Monkeys are broadly classified into two main types: New World monkeys (found in the Americas) and Old World monkeys ( found in Africa and Asia). The tail structure is one of the distinguishing features between these two groups.

Alligators

Alligators are large, semi-aquatic reptiles belonging to the Alligatoridae family. There are two living species of alligators: the American alligator (Alligator mississippiensis) and the Chinese alligator (Alligator sinensis).
